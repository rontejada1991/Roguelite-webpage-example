What are roguelike games?

For a game to be considered roguelike, they need to process most of these qualities:

  • Randomly generated room layouts with every new game.
  • Randomly generated items with every new game. Items will often have vague descriptions on their capabilities.
  • A turn-based combat system.
  • Single-player focused. Some games have implemented a leaderboard system.
  • Once a character dies, the player must begin a new game. Also known as permadeath.

What are hybrid roguelike / roguelike-like / rogue-lite games anyway?

These games inherit a few mechanics often found in roguelike games but also inject their own flavor to create unique experiences.
